## Annual Leave Policy
- Employees are entitled to 18 paid annual leave days per year.
- Leave accrues monthly.
- Maximum 8 unused leave days can be carried forward.
- All leaves require prior approval from reporting manager.

## Sick Leave
- 10 sick leave days per year.
- Medical certificate required for leave more than 2 days.

## Casual Leave
- 6 casual leave days per year.
- Can be taken for personal emergencies.

## Work Policy
- 6 working days per week.
- Flexible learning environment for interns.
- Freshers receive real-time industry exposure.

## Internship Policy
- Internship duration: 3 to 6 months.
- Live project experience provided.
- Performance-based full-time offer possible.
