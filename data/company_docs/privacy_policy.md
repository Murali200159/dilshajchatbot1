# Privacy Policy

We take your data security seriously.

1. **Data Collection**: We collect only the information necessary to provide our services, such as email addresses and basic profiling data.
2. **Usage**: Your data is used exclusively to improve the AI agent's performance and personalize your experience.
3. **Sharing**: We NEVER sell your data to third-party advertisers. 
4. **Security**: All personal data is encrypted using AES-256 at rest and TLS 1.3 in transit.
5. **GDPR/CCPA**: We are fully compliant with GDPR and CCPA. Users can request a full data export or "the right to be forgotten" by contacting privacy@company.com.

For more details, visit the legal section of our website.
